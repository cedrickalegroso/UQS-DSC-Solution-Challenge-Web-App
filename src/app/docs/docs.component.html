About UQS

The problem and how we solved it

Here in the Philippines, queues in banks, schools, and government agencies could consume a lot of time and energy from people. People complain about waiting in long line. In certain cases, there are some who stay in a queue, while wanting to run some errands. But they canâ€™t do them simultaneously. This is a type of a problem that almost every one of us encounter daily. Hence, we created a solution.

When it comes to software for digital queuing, there's a bunch of them out there, but they are specific to one service. We created a Unified digital queuing software, where at a glance, you can see services, and queue without worrying about staying in the vicinity the whole time because it notifies you when your turn is near.

If you are a student like us or someone who needs to pay bills over the counter and want to manage time efficiently, you will surely benefit using UQS. Now you can attend your classes and meeting or go shopping while queuing online, anytime, anywhere and not worry about missing the line. Our goal is simple and straight forward. It is to create an app that provides efficient service not only for people but also for institutions (by helping them provide better quality of service) and make the long waiting in-line a thing in the past.

How the App works

Services will create their account in UQS. Then Users will be able to see the service and can queue on it and also see informations.

Notification Trigger events

The App will notify you using FCM and Email if you enable it. When you created a ticket you should recieve an Email notifying you created a ticket. Then you will recieve another one if your ticket has reached your given trigger. The final Notification Event is when you are next in-line.

Managing Tickets

  1. Register and verify email address then login.
  2. Using the service Dashboard
    1. The tickets you see in the dashboard are tickets without a teller. From here you either delete it or just let it queue.
    2. Click on the Live Queue on the side navigation. This will open a new tab where the tickets with teller are shown.
      • Tickets that don't have a teller will not show in the live queue
      • Every service live queue can be viewed by every user of uqs
    3. Click the teller in the side navigation and choose a teller number. Then click next, this will find the next ticket without a teller.
      • By clicking next it will query a ticket ascending order where the next ticket should have no teller and pending.
      • After transaction is done you can click "done"

For testing porpuse's we have added a form field in your account settings where you can manipulate the ticket counts this will make the app features flexible for testing.

Note: Some parts of the web app is not yet mobile friendly.

Note: The moment that the ticket has a registered teller, it will automatically show up in the live queue.

Note: Some functions/fetaures may take a while. We are using Cloud Functions and it has a cold start sometimes.

Create Ticket

  1. Register and verify email address then login.
  2. Creating the ticket
    1. In the navigation bar click on services then find your service of choice.
    2. Tap on it you will be navigated to the service information from here you can see the live queue or queue.
      • Live queue is where you can see what teller is online.
      • Queue is where you can subscribe to this service.
    3. Tap on queue then select a trigger
      • A trigger is a number where if that is the difference from your ticket number it will notify you.
      • You may see some service's that the trigger is not available this is because there should be atleast 5 tickets pending on the service.
    4. You can enable email notification where you will recieve notification about your ticket through email. An FCM notification will still notify you by default.
    5. After creating a ticket you will see the ticket in Home(Active tickets) from here you can see infrmation's such how many peron's before you, your ticket timeline, and you can also cancel your ticket.
    6. You will be notified if next in the queue.

Note: Some functions/fetaures may take a while. We are using Cloud Functions and it has a cold start sometimes.
